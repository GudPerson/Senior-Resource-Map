## 1-Page Summary Of CareAround SG

CareAround SG is an independently developed web application that helps people in Singapore discover senior-related community resources, save useful options, and organise those options into practical directories and maps for planning, sharing, printing, or follow-up.

The public-facing product serves guests, seniors, caregivers, and family members. It includes resource discovery, resource detail pages, saved resources, My Directory, My Maps, shared maps, language/accessibility foundations, and profile context. The operational product serves partner staff and administrators. It includes dashboard resource management, workbook import/export, service-area and audience-zone controls, translation review, membership links, partner-only notes/files, AI-assisted data preparation, and current branch work for partner organisation staff handover.

CareAround SG is best described as a community-care navigation and planning tool. It is not an emergency service, medical advice platform, clinical triage system, official government directory, or AIC-endorsed product unless formal approval is obtained.

## Why It Was Built

CareAround SG was built to reduce service-navigation friction for seniors, caregivers, families, and community partners. The product addresses a common practical gap: people may know support exists, but still struggle to find nearby, relevant, understandable, current, and shareable information.

The app also gives community partners a structured way to maintain resource listings, review imported or AI-assisted drafts, manage visibility/access rules, and support safer sharing of resource options.

## What It Does Not Claim

CareAround SG does not claim that:

- AIC endorses, sponsors, approves, operates, procures, or recommends the product.
- The product is an official AIC service or government service.
- The product provides medical, legal, financial, emergency, or clinical advice.
- The product diagnoses, triages, ranks clinical need, or replaces professional judgment.
- Resource information is guaranteed complete, current, or suitable for every user.
- AI suggestions are final recommendations.
- Partner-funded or sponsor-funded resources deserve preferential visibility.

## Proposed Safeguards

- Disclose CareAround SG early to the appropriate AIC/HR/compliance contact before any AIC-facing approach.
- Do not claim AIC endorsement, sponsorship, approval, or operational involvement.
- Do not use AIC logos, confidential materials, private contacts, internal templates, or non-public datasets without written permission.
- Keep CareAround SG communications on independent channels and branding.
- Keep seniors/caregivers free to use the public product.
- Do not monetise ranking or placement in sensitive community-care search results without a reviewed disclosure policy.
- Recuse from any AIC, employer, or partner decision that could affect CareAround SG.
- Keep pilot partners, funders, and advisors documented.
- Use public/demo-safe data for screenshots, decks, and pilots unless a data-sharing agreement permits otherwise.
- Keep AI support review-first, explainable, and clearly non-clinical.
- Add audit logs and consent records before analytics, external notifications, or AI-assisted support matching.
